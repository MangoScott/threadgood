The rating

Four things that matter

Every brand is graded A through F on four dimensions. Simple enough to understand at a glance. Deep enough to trust.

Dimension 1
WHERE
Supply chain transparency. Where are your clothes made? How much of the chain is disclosed? We use US Customs data to verify.
Dimension 2
WHO
Labor practices and worker welfare. Living wages, safety records, forced labor risk. Weighted heaviest because people matter most.
Dimension 3
WHAT
Materials, chemicals, and durability. What fibers are in your clothes? Are they safe? Will they last?
Dimension 4
AFTER
Circularity and end-of-life. Can it be recycled? Does the brand take it back? What about the packaging?

The data

Built on data nobody else uses

AI-powered analysis of US federal databases that global platforms don't touch. Updated continuously, not annually.

⚓
US Customs Records
Import data reveals where clothes really come from, who ships them, and in what volumes.
⚠
OSHA Violations
Workplace safety inspections, violations, and penalties for US facilities.
🌱
EPA Enforcement
Environmental compliance, chemical releases, and enforcement actions.
⚖
FTC Complaints
Greenwashing settlements, deceptive labeling, and false "Made in USA" claims.
📈
SEC Filings
ESG disclosures, risk factors, and supply chain reports from public companies.
💰
DOL Wage Data
Minimum wage violations, overtime theft, and back-wage enforcement records.
🏭
Open Apparel Registry
Verified factory-to-brand connections across the global supply chain.
📑
CA Supply Chain Act
Legally required disclosures on slavery and trafficking in supply chains.
