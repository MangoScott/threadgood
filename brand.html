        How is this score calculated?

        ThreadGrade uses an independent algorithm to strip away "greenwashing" and grade brands strictly on publicly verified evidence across three global pillars:

        • Planet (45%): Analyzes supply chain transparency (tier 1-3 factory disclosure), volume of verified sustainable materials (e.g. recycled polyester), microplastics mitigation policies, and complete garment circularity (take-back/resale programs).
        • People (40%): Evaluates verified payment of living wages (not just minimum wage), freedom of association, un-redacted supplier audit releases, and active scrutiny against government databases (like OSHA safety records and CBP Forced Labor flags).
        • Animals (15%): Tracks robust animal welfare certifications (Responsible Down Standard, ZQ Merino), outright bans on exotic skins and fur, and investments in vegan innovations to reduce biosphere dependencies.

        Missing Data Policy: If a brand refuses to publicly publish any verifiable supply chain or tracking data, ThreadGrade refuses to assign them a passing score and instead legally labels them as Unranked (NR). Brands must provide transparent, public certifications to be officially graded on our scale, and they will immediately dive to a D or F if they trigger an OSHA or CBP Forced Labor penalty.
